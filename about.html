Ever since UW-Eau Claire was founded back in 1916, Blugold Dining has been committed to feeding its hardworking students, faculty, and campus visitors. From the very beginning, the quality of both the food and service is priority number one to deliver a pleasent dining experience.

The campus dining experience has evolved over the years, going from a few basic food options to expanding with more food options that include popular franchise cafes and restaurants. We are continuously adapting to food trends and styles to ensure customers can find exactly what they are craving.

If you have any questions, comments, recommendations, or concerns please feel free to contact Blugold Dining at dining@uwec.edu or by calling at 715-836-5261.
