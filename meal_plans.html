Meal plans allow students and faculty to eat at a variety of locations on campus everyday. Each meal plan has different benefits, so read them all to find one that is best for you.

There are four meal plans for on campus students and three meal plans that are geared toward off campus students. Students living on campus in Chancellors Hall are not required to buy a meal plan, but students living in all other resident halls are required to have a meal plan.

On Campus Meal Plans

Price: $1,495

Dine on both upper and lower campus.

On upper campus, students have access to the Hilltop Riverview Cafe everyday, including weekends. This is a buffet style location so students may choose any food offered. There is also the option to get one to-go meal per day before 8pm. To-go meal containers require an additional $5.00 at time of meal plan purchase.

On lower campus, one block meal may be used per day in Davies center (including The Cabin) or at Einstein Bros. Bagels in Centenial Hall.

50 meal plan dollars are included with this plan. Meal plan dollars can be used at any of the on campus dining locations.

Price: $1,400

Dine on both upper and lower campus.

Monday through Friday there are two block meals available per day and can be used on upper or lower campus. Upper-Campus block meals are used at Hilltop Riverview Cafe and Lower-Campus blocks can be used at Davies Center or Einstein Bros. Bagels in Centenial Hall.

From Friday at 4pm until Sunday at 8pm, students may go as often as they wish to Hilltop Riverview Cafe.

50 meal plan dollars are included with this plan. Meal plan dollars can be used at any of the on campus dining locations.

Price: $1,400

Dine on both upper and lower campus.

This meal offers 1,080 meal plan dollars that can be used at any on campus location.

Generally, first year students do not get this plan nor does Blugold dining recommend this plan for first year students. It is still available, but incoming students find a meal plan with block meals much more convenient.

Off Campus/Other Meal Plans

Price: $150

Dine on both upper and lower campus.

On upper campus you can dine at Hilltop Riverview Cafe.

On lower campus you can dine at Davies center marketplace, The Cabin in Davies, Einstein Bros. Bagels in Centenial Hall.

Block meals purchased through this plan will only expire once you leave the university.

Purchase this meal plan at the Blugold Card Services office located in Davies Center.
